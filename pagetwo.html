Emmanuel Adebayo: "They used to hear and read it from the newspapers. This time round

they have seen it and I am sure they now believe that AFCON is as good as the EURO and even better than the nations league and copa America. When a player is called up by his national team, let no one stop them by saying AFCON is nothing."
